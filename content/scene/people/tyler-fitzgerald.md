Bass-baritone Tyler Fitzgerald has performed with many of the major opera companies and symphonies across Canada, and in 2016 made his European debut as Count Ceprano in *Rigoletto* with the Staatstheater in Mainz. Recent performances include: concerts with the Calgary Bach Society, Alberto in *Figaro's Wedding* (Cowtown Opera), Masetto (*Don Giovanni*) with Edmonton Opera, and Stevens in Rattenbury with Pacific Opera Victoria.
